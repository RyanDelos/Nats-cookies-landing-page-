The Story of Nat's Cookies

It all started in High School during Nat's culinary class, where she gained inspiration by a baking project. With the spark of inspiration, came action to baking her own cookies, and shared them with her peers. The feedback was amazing! Family and friends suggested she start her own cookie business. Aiming to reach a broader audience, Nat uploaded her creation on social media where word spread like wildfire. Nat's cookies was born and is currently featured selling her cookies on a food truck today!
