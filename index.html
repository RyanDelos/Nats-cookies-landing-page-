The Story of Nat's Cookies

It all started in High School during Nat's culinary class, where she gained inspiration by a baking project. With the spark of inspiration, came action to baking her own cookies, and shared them with her peers. The feedback was amazing! Friends and family suggested to start her own cookie business. Reaching a broader audience Nat uploaded her creation on social media where word spread like wild fire. Nat's cookies was born and is currently featured selling her cookies on a food truck today!
